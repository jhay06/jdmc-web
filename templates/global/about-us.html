ABOUT US

Company Profile

JMDC Imaging Center is part of Jagas Medicadenta Corporation. We are also located at Jagas Building #46 6th St. Filipinas Avenue, UPS 5, Parañaque Subdivision, Sucat, Parañaque, 1713 Metro Manila. JMDC Imaging Center team and/or organization focus on providing solutions with a modern digital equipments used by radiologists and dental professionals for various clinical applications including dental implant planning, visualization of abnormal teeth, evaluation of jaws and face, cleft palate assessment, diagnosis of dental caries (cavities) endodontic (root canal) diagnosis, diagnosis of dental trauma.

Mission

Our mission is to provide compassionate, caring, diagnose and create a surgical plan for a variety of procedures and/or the most effective diagnostic imaging technology with high-tech medical facility that will allow them to enjoy safe, long-lasting and comfortable to improve the quality of life.

Vision

Our vision to establish nationally and internationally recognized as an outstanding for maintaining the highest level of patients care satisfaction and comfort.
